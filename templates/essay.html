💡 Essay Writing Tips

Start collecting material NOW. Every time you finish a task and add a note, you're building raw material for your essays. By summer 2027, you'll have dozens of specific moments, numbers, and reflections to draw from — instead of staring at a blank page trying to remember what you did.

Numbers tell stories. "I raised money" is forgettable. "I played 30 gigs, raised $10,000, and put instruments in the hands of 20 kids" is unforgettable. Track everything.

The best essays show transformation. Not "I did X" but "X changed how I see Y." Your weekly reflections capture these shifts in real time.
